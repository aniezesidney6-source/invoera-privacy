1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve the Invoera service:

Account Information

Your name, email address, and password when you create an Invoera account.

Business Information

Business name, address, phone number, website, logo, bank account details, and other information you enter in your business profile.

Invoice & Transaction Data

Invoices you create, customer names and contact details, payment amounts, item descriptions, VAT, discounts, and invoice history.

Device Information

Device type, operating system, app version, and push notification tokens to deliver in-app alerts.

Usage Data

How you interact with the app — features used, screens visited, and actions taken — to help us improve your experience.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your Invoera account
  • To generate, store, and share invoices on your behalf
  • To send payment reminders and push notifications you have enabled
  • To process Pro plan subscriptions via Google Play
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • To improve app features, fix bugs, and enhance performance
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations in Nigeria
🔒

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not display advertisements inside the Invoera app.

3 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with enterprise-grade security. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS and encrypted at rest.

  • Your password is never stored in plain text — it is securely hashed
  • Bank account details are stored in your private, access-controlled database record
  • Row-level security policies ensure you can only ever access your own data
  • We follow industry-standard security practices to protect against unauthorised access

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and enable biometric lock for added protection.

4 Sharing of Information

We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), Expo (app platform), and Google Play (billing) — each bound by their own privacy policies
  • Legal Requirements: If required by Nigerian law, a court order, or a regulatory authority
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the new owner
  • Your Consent: For any other purpose with your explicit permission

We do not share your invoice data, customer information, or business details with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

5 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information at any time in the app settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Export your invoice data as a CSV file directly from the app
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

6 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your Invoera account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently remove your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain records for legal or regulatory compliance.

Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for product improvement purposes.

7 Children's Privacy

Invoera is designed for business use by adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

8 Push Notifications

Invoera may send push notifications for invoice reminders, payment alerts, and important account updates. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

We do not use push notifications for marketing or promotional messages.

9 Third-Party Services

Invoera integrates with the following services. Please review their privacy policies for details on how they handle your data:

  • Supabase — database and authentication · supabase.com/privacy
  • Google Play — app distribution and billing · policies.google.com/privacy
  • Expo — app platform and push notifications · expo.dev/privacy
  • WhatsApp — only used when you explicitly share an invoice via WhatsApp

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email. Your continued use of Invoera after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
